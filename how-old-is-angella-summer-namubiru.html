Biography of Angella Summer Namubiru

Angella Summer Namubiru was born on December 22, 1988, in Kampala, Uganda. She grew up in a musical family, which greatly influenced her passion for singing and performing. Angella's early exposure to music allowed her to develop her talent from a young age.

Angella was raised in a supportive environment where music was an integral part of her upbringing. She began singing in church choirs and participating in local talent shows, showcasing her vocal abilities. This early involvement in music laid the foundation for her future career.
