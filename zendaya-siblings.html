Biography of Zendaya

Zendaya Maree Stoermer Coleman, popularly known as Zendaya, was born on September 1, 1996, in Oakland, California. She is the daughter of Claire Stoermer and Kazembe Ajamu Coleman, with her father being a former teacher and her mother a stage manager. Zendaya's career began in the world of dance and theater, leading her to become a household name in the entertainment industry.
