Biography of Ice Spice

Ice Spice, born as Isis Gaston on January 1, 2000, is a talented rapper and songwriter hailing from the Bronx, New York. She gained significant attention in 2022 with her debut single “Munch (Feelin' U),” which became a viral sensation on social media platforms.

Ice Spice's journey began in her teenage years, where she would often create music in her free time.
