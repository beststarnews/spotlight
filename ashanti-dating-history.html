Biography of Ashanti

Ashanti Shequoiya Douglas, better known by her stage name Ashanti, was born on October 13, 1980, in Glen Cove, New York. She is an accomplished singer, songwriter, and actress, recognized for her unique blend of R&B and hip-hop music. Ashanti gained fame in the early 2000s with hits like "Foolish" and "Baby," which showcased her powerful vocals and emotional depth.
