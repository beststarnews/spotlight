Biography of Megan Thee Stallion

Megan Thee Stallion, born Megan Jovon Ruth Pete on February 15, 1995, in San Antonio, Texas, is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. She grew up in Houston, where she began rapping at the age of 16. Her mother, Holly Thomas, was also a rapper, which influenced Megan's passion for music from an early age. After her mother's passing, Megan became even more determined to pursue her dreams in the music industry.
