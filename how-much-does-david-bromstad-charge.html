Biography of David Bromstad

David Bromstad was born on August 17, 1973, in Cokato, Minnesota. He developed a passion for art and design at a young age, leading him to pursue a degree in Fine Arts from the Ringling College of Art and Design in Sarasota, Florida. After graduating, he worked as a custom furniture designer before gaining national fame on HGTV.

His big break came in 2006 when he won "HGTV Design Star," which led to his own show, "Color Splash." Since then, he has hosted several other shows, including "My Lottery Dream Home" and "Beach Flip," further establishing himself as a household name in the realm of home renovation and design.
