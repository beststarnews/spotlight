Biography of Tiffany Haddish

Tiffany Sara Haddish was born on December 3, 1979, in Los Angeles, California. Raised in a challenging environment, her childhood experiences greatly influenced her comedic style and perspective. After facing numerous hardships, including the loss of her father and struggles with her mother’s mental health, she found solace in comedy and performing arts. Tiffany's unique blend of humor and authenticity quickly garnered attention.
