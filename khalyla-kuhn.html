Khalyla's upbringing was marked by a blend of cultures that enriched her formative years. Her father is of Filipino descent, while her mother is of American descent. She graduated with a degree in Biochemistry from the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA). Career Path

Khalyla's career journey began in the field of education and science, but she soon discovered her passion for entertainment and storytelling.
