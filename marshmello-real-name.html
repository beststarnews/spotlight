What is Marshmello's Real Name?

The real name of Marshmello is Christopher Comstock. Initially gaining fame as a producer under the moniker Dotcom, Comstock adopted the Marshmello persona to create a more approachable and relatable image. His decision to wear a marshmallow helmet allows him to maintain a level of anonymity, a strategy that has proven successful in the music industry.
