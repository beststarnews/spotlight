Janet Guzman: The Rise Of A Social Media Sensation

Biography of Janet Guzman

Janet Guzman was born on December 24, 1993, in Los Angeles, California. She is of Mexican descent and has always embraced her cultural heritage. Janet showed an early interest in fashion and beauty, often experimenting with different styles and makeup looks. Her passion for creativity led her to pursue a career in social media influencing, where she could share her talents with a broader audience.
