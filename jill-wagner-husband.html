Biography of Jill Wagner

Jill Wagner, born on January 13, 1979, in Winston-Salem, North Carolina, is an accomplished actress, model, and television personality. She first gained fame through her appearances on the hit series "Blade: The Series," where her performance as Krista Star earned her recognition in the entertainment industry. Over the years, Jill has become a staple in Hallmark films, showcasing her versatility as an actress.
