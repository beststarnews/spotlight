Biography of YoungBoy Never Broke Again

YoungBoy Never Broke Again, born Kentrell Gaulden on October 20, 1999, in Baton Rouge, Louisiana, is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. He gained prominence in the music industry with his mixtapes and singles, particularly "Outside Today," which peaked at number 31 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart. YoungBoy's music often reflects his tumultuous upbringing and personal struggles, resonating with a wide audience.

How Many Kids Does YoungBoy Have?

As of now, YoungBoy Never Broke Again has a total of six children. His journey as a father has been multifaceted, with each child representing a different chapter in his life. The following sections will provide insights into each of his children and their respective mothers.

Details About His First Child

YoungBoy's first child, a son named Kayden Gaulden, was born on July 4, 2016. Kayden's mother is Nisha, a woman with whom YoungBoy had a brief relationship. YoungBoy frequently posts about Kayden, showcasing their bond and the joys of fatherhood.

Details About His Second Child

His second child, a son named Kacey Alexander Gaulden, was born on February 13, 2019. Kacey's mother is Jania Bania, who has been in a tumultuous relationship with YoungBoy. Their on-and-off relationship has been well-documented in the media, but YoungBoy maintains a close relationship with Kacey.

Details About His Third Child

YoungBoy welcomed his third child, a daughter named Armani, with his ex-girlfriend, Yaya Mayweather, in January 2020. Despite the challenges that come with co-parenting, YoungBoy has expressed his commitment to being a responsible father to Armani.
