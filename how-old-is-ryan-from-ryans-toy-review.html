Ryan's Biography

Ryan Kaji, widely known as Ryan, was born on October 6, 2011. He rose to fame through his YouTube channel, Ryan's Toy Review, which he launched at the age of just three. The channel features Ryan unboxing and reviewing a variety of toys, engaging in fun activities, and sharing family-friendly content. Over the years, Ryan has become a prominent figure in the children’s entertainment industry, amassing millions of followers and a significant online presence.

How Old is Ryan?

As of 2023, Ryan Kaji is 12 years old. His birthday, celebrated on October 6, marks another year of his journey in the entertainment industry. Ryan's youthful charm and infectious enthusiasm have contributed significantly to his popularity, making him one of the highest-earning YouTubers in the world.

Ryan's Age Milestones

  • 2011: Ryan Kaji is born.

History of Ryan's Toy Review

Ryan's Toy Review began as a simple concept where a child reviews toys, but it quickly evolved into a phenomenon. Launched in 2015, the channel gained traction with its engaging content and relatable personality. Ryan's authentic reactions and playful demeanor resonated with young viewers, leading to a rapid increase in subscribers.

Key Highlights in Channel History

  • 2015: Ryan's Toy Review is launched.
  • 2016: The channel gains over 1 million subscribers.
  • 2018: Ryan becomes the highest-paid YouTuber according to Forbes.
  • 2020: Ryan's World, a brand extension, is launched, including toys, clothing, and games.

Ryan and His Family

Ryan's success is not just a solo endeavor; it involves his entire family. His parents, Loann and Shion Kaji, play a crucial role in the channel's content and management. They ensure that the videos are not only entertaining but also safe and appropriate for children.

Family Involvement
