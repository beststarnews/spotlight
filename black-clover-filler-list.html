What is Filler in Anime?

In the world of anime, "filler" refers to episodes that do not contribute to the main plot of the series. These episodes are often created to allow the manga to progress further ahead of the anime adaptation. Filler episodes can feature side stories, character backstories, or humorous adventures that do not impact the overall narrative. While some fans enjoy these episodes for their character development and world-building, others prefer to focus exclusively on the canonical story.
