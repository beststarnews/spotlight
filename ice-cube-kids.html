Biography of Ice Cube Kids

Ice Cube has four children with his wife, Kimberly Woodruff. Their names are O'Shea Jr., Darrell, Shareef, and Karima. Growing up in a household that values education and hard work, each child has found their unique path while staying connected to their father's influence.

O'Shea Jackson Jr.

Born on February 24, 1991, O'Shea Jackson Jr. is the eldest son of Ice Cube. He has followed in his father's footsteps in the entertainment industry, making his acting debut in the film "Straight Outta Compton," where he portrayed his father. His performance was widely praised, showcasing his talent and dedication to his craft.

Darrell Jackson

Darrell, born on December 29, 1992, has also ventured into the entertainment industry but has focused more on music. He has showcased his skills as a rapper and songwriter, embracing the legacy of his father while carving out his own identity in the hip-hop scene.

Shareef Jackson

Shareef was born on November 17, 1995. He is known for his athleticism and has played basketball at the collegiate level. Shareef has a passion for sports and fitness, representing the importance of pursuing one's interests and passions.

Karima Jackson

Born on February 4, 1994, Karima is the youngest of Ice Cube's children. She has shown interest in fashion and entrepreneurship, often working on projects that reflect her creativity and business acumen. Karima's journey highlights the importance of being true to oneself.
