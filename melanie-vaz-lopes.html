Biography of Melanie Vaz Lopes

Melanie Vaz Lopes was born on April 15, 1995, in Lisbon, Portugal. She grew up in a vibrant environment that encouraged creativity and self-expression. From a young age, Melanie showed a keen interest in fashion, beauty, and photography, which later became the cornerstones of her digital content.
