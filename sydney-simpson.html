Biography of Sydney Simpson

Sydney Brooke Simpson was born on October 17, 1994, in Los Angeles, California. She is the younger sister of Justin Ryan Simpson and the daughter of O.J. Simpson and Nicole Brown Simpson. Sydney was just a child when her mother was tragically murdered in 1994, an event that not only changed her life forever but also became one of the most publicized criminal trials in American history.
