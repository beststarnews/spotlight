Biography of Mimi from Love and Hip Hop

Mimi Faust, widely known as Mimi from Love and Hip Hop, was born on January 1, 1972, in Los Angeles, California. She is an American reality television personality, businesswoman, and actress. Mimi gained fame through her appearances on the VH1 reality series, Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta, which premiered in 2012. Her storyline often revolves around her relationships, particularly with her long-term partner, Stevie J, and their daughter, Eva.
