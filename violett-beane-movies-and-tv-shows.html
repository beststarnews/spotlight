Biography of Violett Beane

Violett Beane was born on May 18, 1996, in St. Petersburg, Florida, USA. She grew up in a creative environment that fostered her passion for the arts. Beane's journey into acting began at a young age, and she quickly gained recognition for her talent and dedication.

Early Career

Violett Beane's acting career began with small roles in various television series and short films. Her breakthrough came with her role as Jesse Wells in the hit series "The Flash," where she showcased her exceptional acting skills. This role opened doors for Beane, allowing her to take on more significant projects in both film and television.

During her early career, Violett Beane also appeared in other popular shows, including "The Mindy Project" and "Roadies," where she demonstrated her versatility as an actress. Her performances in these series garnered attention from critics and audiences alike.

Notable Movies

Throughout her career, Violett Beane has starred in several notable movies, each showcasing her range as an actress. Here are some of the significant films she has appeared in:

  • Flay (2017) - In this horror film, Violett plays the role of a young woman who must confront her past in order to survive.
  • The Last Word (2017) - Starring alongside legends like Shirley MacLaine, Beane portrays a young journalist who helps an elderly woman write her memoir.
  • Truth or Dare (2018) - In this supernatural horror film, Violett takes on the role of a college student who participates in a deadly game.
  • Uncaged (2016) - Violett plays a key role in this thriller about a group of friends who encounter a terrifying creature.

Television Shows

In addition to her film work, Violett Beane has made significant contributions to television. Some of her noteworthy television appearances include:
