Biography of Lenny Kravitz

Lenny Kravitz was born to a mixed-race family in New York City. His father, Sy Kravitz, was a Jewish television producer, while his mother, Roxie Roker, was an African-American actress known for her role on the television show "The Jeffersons." Growing up in a multicultural environment, Lenny was exposed to a variety of musical styles and artistic expressions that would later influence his own work.
