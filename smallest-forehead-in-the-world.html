What is Considered a Small Forehead?

A small forehead is generally characterized by a reduced distance from the hairline to the eyebrows. While beauty standards vary across cultures, the average forehead size is typically around 5 to 6 inches in height. A forehead measuring less than 4 inches can be considered small. However, it is essential to remember that beauty is subjective, and many factors influence perceptions of attractiveness.

Measurement of Forehead Size

  • Forehead height: The vertical distance from the hairline to the brow line.
  • Forehead width: The horizontal distance from one temple to the other.
  • Proportionality: The forehead size in relation to other facial features.
