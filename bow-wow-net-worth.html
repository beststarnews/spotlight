Biography of Bow Wow

Bow Wow, born Shad Gregory Moss on March 9, 1987, in Columbus, Ohio, began his career in music at a young age. He was discovered by rapper Snoop Dogg, who introduced him to the music industry. Bow Wow released his debut album, "Beware of Dog," when he was just 13 years old, which set the stage for his future success.
