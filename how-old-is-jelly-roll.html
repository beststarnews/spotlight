Biography of Jelly Roll

Jelly Roll was born on December 4, 1986, in Nashville, Tennessee. Growing up in a challenging environment, he faced various adversities that shaped his character and music. His upbringing in a rough neighborhood exposed him to the realities of life, which later became a recurring theme in his songs.

Jelly Roll's Music Career

Jelly Roll's music career began in the early 2010s when he started releasing mixtapes and collaborating with various artists. His breakthrough came with the release of his single, "Save Me," which garnered millions of views on platforms like YouTube.
