Biography of Michael Imperioli

Michael Imperioli was born on March 26, 1976, in Mount Vernon, New York. He is best known for his role as Christopher Moltisanti in the critically acclaimed TV series "The Sopranos," which aired from 1999 to 2007. Michael's performance earned him several accolades, including an Emmy Award for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Drama Series.

Before his fame on "The Sopranos," Michael had various roles in theater and independent films, establishing himself as a talented actor with a passion for storytelling. His career has spanned over two decades, with numerous projects in film, television, and theater. Michael is also a writer and director, showcasing his versatility in the entertainment industry.
