Biography of Ila Kreischer

Ila Kreischer was born on June 29, 1997, in Los Angeles, California. She is the daughter of Bert Kreischer, a well-known stand-up comedian, and LeeAnn Kreischer. Growing up in a creative environment, Ila was exposed to the world of comedy and entertainment from a young age. Her father's success undoubtedly influenced her aspirations, but Ila has worked hard to establish her own identity in the industry.
