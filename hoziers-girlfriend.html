Biography of Hozier

Andrew Hozier-Byrne, known professionally as Hozier, was born on March 17, 1990, in Bray, County Wicklow, Ireland. His upbringing in a musical family played a significant role in shaping his passion for music. Hozier attended Trinity College Dublin, where he studied music and earned a reputation as a talented musician. His big break came in 2013 with the release of "Take Me to Church," which earned him Grammy nominations and propelled him to international fame.
