Biography of Megan Thee Stallion

Megan Thee Stallion, whose real name is Megan Jovon Ruth Pete, was born on February 15, 1995, in San Antonio, Texas. She later moved to Houston, where she was raised by her mother, who was a rapper herself. This environment played a crucial role in shaping Megan's passion for music.
