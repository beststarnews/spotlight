Biography of Chioma Adeleke

Chioma Adeleke is a Nigerian singer and songwriter, known for her unique sound and captivating performances. Born on December 15, 2000, in Lagos, Nigeria, Chioma is currently 23 years old. She hails from a family of musicians, which greatly influenced her passion for music from a young age. Chioma began singing in church and participated in various local talent shows, where she honed her skills and gained confidence on stage.
