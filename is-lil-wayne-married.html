Biography of Lil Wayne

Born Dwayne Michael Carter Jr. on September 27, 1982, in New Orleans, Louisiana, Lil Wayne rose to fame as one of the most influential figures in the hip-hop industry. He began rapping at a young age and gained recognition as a member of the Hot Boys, a hip-hop group in the late 1990s. Over the years, he has released numerous critically acclaimed albums and has won multiple awards, including Grammy Awards.

Lil Wayne's Marriages

Lil Wayne has been married once. He tied the knot with his childhood sweetheart, Toya Johnson, in 2004. The couple welcomed a daughter, Reginae Carter, in 1998, before getting married. However, their marriage was short-lived, and they divorced in 2006. Despite their separation, they have maintained a cordial relationship for the sake of their daughter.
