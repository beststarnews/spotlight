Biography of the Tallest Soccer Player

The title of the tallest soccer player ever goes to Kristof Van Hout, a Belgian goalkeeper who stands at an astonishing height of 2.08 meters (6 feet 10 inches). Born on January 6, 1987, in Neerpelt, Belgium, Van Hout has made a name for himself in the world of soccer, particularly in the Belgian Pro League.
